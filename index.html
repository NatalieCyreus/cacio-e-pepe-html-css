Cacio e pepe

Cacio e pepe (Italian pronunciation: [kaˈtʃo e ˈpeːpe]) is a pasta dish from modern Roman cuisine."Cacio e pepe" means "cheese and pepper" in several central Italian dialects. As the name suggests, the ingredients of the dish are very simple: black pepper, grated Pecorino Romano cheese, and spaghetti,or traditionally tonnarelli.

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • butter
  • black pepper
  • pecorino cheese
